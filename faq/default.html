A: [Many courts will allow this but if there are any questions give the contact person your information to who is ordering you to a program and let them check for you]

A: [No unless you express consent to it.  Being annoymous is part of this program.  But as you grow and move thru these steps, letting others know things you have learned and things you went thru might help know they aren't alone and feel more comfortable in working these steps.  But nothing talked about in group will ever be spoken or posted without written consent.  **The only exceptions to this are if you talk about hurting yourself or others.  ]
