About Rejoice and Recovery

   Rejoice and Recovery is based in Doyle , Tennessee but reaches out to surrounding county's.  The men and women of R and R also reach out to help our community by bringing 12 steps to the inmates of White County Justice Center. This 12 Step Group  meets every Tuesday at 6 pm at Doyle Methodists Church.  It is a faith based Program that bodly leads people to recovery thru 12 Steps of AA , love and understanding.

 12 Steps of AA and NA been around and proven that by taking an inventory of yourself and with the help from other recovering addicts ,we can overcome this monster that many call ADDICTION.  It will be important that as you begin this that you take on the mind frame of a child.  As a child we were like a sponge and easily take in information and accept it.  As a child we weren't bound by science or things we could see.  A child was open to being with and around others for support.  So before you begin each day and step, please open your heart and mind and spirit to an awakening of such proportions that you thought were never possible.  Stay positive and know that some of these steps can be a challenge and maybe even bring you back to places that hurt you, but don't get discouraged or let that pain keep you in this prison of being out of control.  Coping with these very fears can be your avenue of success in recovery.  Stay positive! 

  Through out this site you will not only read pages from the Blue Book of AA  and NA, but also real life testimonies and suggestions from people overcoming addiction just as we are.  You're not alone and that's why we are bringing 12 steps to you this way.  Some may not be able to get to meetings, so by the grace of GOD, my higher power, we are bringing meetings to you in a way like never before.  We will remain and keep our privacy, as our recovery is no ones business.  We, Addicts are not trash, we are just broken and in need of guidance for repair.  Think about it this way, if your car is broken are you going to take it to the cart pusher at your grocery store?  NO, you take it to a mechanic who has the knowledge and skills needed to repair your car!  This 12 step program is comprised of recovering addicts and 12 steps and traditions that have proven to be effective.  We pride ourselves on our recovery and not ashamed of where we have been, but come to a place of forgiveness, so we can begin a repair process and take pride in everyday of sobriety.

  If today is your first day or your 50th year, we welcome you and love you!
